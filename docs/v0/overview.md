## v0 Features Overview

Here are the features implemented in v0, explained simply:

---

### 1. User Authentication (Sign Up, Sign In, Verification)
- **What it does:** Lets users create accounts, log in, and verify their email.
- **Why it was needed:** To make sure only real users can use the app and keep their data safe.
- **How it helps:** Users can have personal playlists and secure access. The system can track users and prevent fake accounts.

---

### 2. Playlist Management (Create, View, Delete Playlists)
- **What it does:** Allows users to make, see, and remove their own playlists.
- **Why it was needed:** So users can organize their favorite songs and listen to them easily.
- **How it helps:** Users get a personalized music experience. The system can store and manage user preferences.

---

### 3. Song Upload (Admin Only)
- **What it does:** Lets admins upload new songs to the app.
- **Why it was needed:** To keep the music library fresh and updated.
- **How it helps:** Users always have new songs to listen to. Admins can manage content easily.

---

### 4. Music Player (Play, Pause, Next, Previous, Progress Bar)
- **What it does:** Lets users play songs, pause, skip, and see song progress.
- **Why it was needed:** To give users control over their listening experience.
- **How it helps:** Users can enjoy music smoothly and interact with the player as they like.

---

### 5. Responsive UI (Header, Navbar, Loading Skeleton)
- **What it does:** Shows a clean interface with navigation and loading indicators.
- **Why it was needed:** To make the app easy to use and look good on all devices.
- **How it helps:** Users can find features quickly and know when something is loading.

---
